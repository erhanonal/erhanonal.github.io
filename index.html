About Our App

MedicaKnow app can easily extract the information about the medication you are using

MedicaKnow

People often feel the need to search information about the medication and dietary supplements they are using. They wonder about the possible side effects and dosage information, but is difficult to reach easy to understand information about a medication. The information pamphlet that comes with a medication is often written in a complicated manner with a lot of scientific terms. Additionally if is often written with a very small font which makes it harder for elderly to read the information. The kind of information you may find online is often a direct copy from the drug information pamphlet which is not particularly useful for a regular user.

We are proposing a mobile application which can take the name of the medication via camera or user input and show the relevant information about the medication like side effects and dosage information in an understandable and a summarized manner for the average user.
